Inventory Management System

The Inventory Management System is a web-based application designed to help businesses efficiently manage their inventory. The system allows users to add, update, delete, and monitor products while maintaining accurate stock records. It aims to simplify inventory tracking and improve operational efficiency.

Project Overview

This project was developed to automate inventory management processes by providing an organized platform for storing product information, monitoring stock availability, and generating inventory records. It reduces manual work and helps maintain accurate inventory data.

Project Objectives

  • Manage inventory efficiently through a centralized system.
  • Add, update, search, and delete product records.
  • Monitor stock availability in real time.
  • Reduce manual inventory management errors.
  • Improve data organization and accessibility.
  • Provide a simple and user-friendly interface.
